Welcome to the Armstrong Numbers App!

Armstrong number (also known as narcissistic, pluperfect, or pluperfect digital invariant) is a number that is the sum of its own digits each raised to the power of the number of digits.

Explore the app to search for Armstrong numbers and check if a specific number is an Armstrong number.
